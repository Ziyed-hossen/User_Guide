Channel Mix Overview
====================

The Channel Analysis reports provide a high-level overview of bookings and activity across eCommerce booking channels. This report provides room night and revenue information for all Channels - GDS, OTA, and/or Website along with percent growth.

Hilton
------

- Data pulled from the Hilton Reporting Hub or the Hilton Tableau CSV.
- Key channels include Web, Voice, GDS, OTA, Property Direct, WS/FIT, and others.

Hyatt
-----

- Channels mapped from PAW reports through Hyatt Connect.

Marriott
--------

- Data is pulled from MRDW ECM1/CBD1.

IHG
---

- Comes from HRI reports.
